Waffle rules

In this game you must solve the WAFFLE in 15 moves or less. Rearrange the letters into the correct words, horizontally and vertically. Drag letters anywhere on the board. The letters will change colour to show whether they are in the correct position. The number of moves remaining is displayed below the board.

The color of each tile represents their relationship to the final row of the grid:

  • Green letter matches the final row exactly.
  • Yellow letter is in the final row, but in a different column.
  • Grey letter is absent from the final row.
  • For repeat letters, only the appropriate number of tiles will be colored, in order of exact matches then left to right.

NOTE: Every WAFFLE Wordle Game can be solved in 10 moves. You will earn a star for every move you have remaining once the WAFFLE is solved.
